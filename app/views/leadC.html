Step C: Reminder email

This is the email that is sent to a respondent if they have not opened your email three days after they received the original "download" email.

Email content Subject [Just a small reminder] How your email will appear in people's inbox. Keep it short and sweet. Headline [Hey!] Your chance to grab attention.
